Ponziani Opening

The Ponziani Opening is a chess opening that begins with the moves: 1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nc6 3. c3

3 ... Bc5

The move Bc5 is a mistake because it runs directly into 4. d4, making the black player into trading 4 ... exd4 5 cxd4 and giving a helpful check on 5. ... Bb4+ 6. Nc3 or retreating back to Bb6, a waste of tempo.
